CUSTOMIZATION OPTIONS
================================================================================

plot_hazard_progression():
  - num_samples: Number of trajectories to plot (default: 5)
  - sample_indices: Specific samples to show
  - save_path: Where to save the figure

plot_oti_policy():
  - cost_values: List of costs to show thresholds for
  - num_samples: Number of samples to analyze
  - save_path: Where to save the figure

plot_policy_tradeoff():
  - test_metrics_list: Metrics from different experiments
  - labels: Labels for each policy/experiment
  - save_path: Where to save the figure
